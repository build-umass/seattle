Special Nordic Youth Concert

Come out and hear this amazing Seattle-based group of young musicians who are carrying forward the Nordic folk music tradition! This Special Nordic Youth Concert is co-sponsored by the Seattle Lilla Spelmanslag and Seattle Folklore Society.

Yuletide End Concert

We were honored to play at the Seattle Bergen Sister City Association’s Yuletide End Concert! If you missed it, catcher us live at the Skandia Folkdance Society's third Friday dance, January 19, 2018 at the Cedar Valley Grange. We open the evening at 8:30 PM. Come out and dance!

10th Anniversary Season

We are ready for our first show of this, our 10th Anniversary Season. We will be playing at the Skandia Ball on Saturday , October 21, at the Latvian Hall.
