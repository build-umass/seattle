Special Nordic Youth Concert

Come out and hear this amazing Seattle-based group of young musicians who are carrying forward the Nordic folk music tradition! This Special Nordic Youth Concert is co-sponsored by the Seattle Lilla Spelmanslag and Seattle Folklore Society.
